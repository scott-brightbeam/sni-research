Welcome to all the AI news that matters this week – across biopharma, medtech, complex manufacturing and insurance.

tl;dr: The funding paradox

The gap between AI investment and AI returns widened dramatically this week. OpenAI's $110bn raise – the largest in AI history – landed alongside Merck's pivot to AI-first drug discovery, Siemens scaling digital twins across a dozen factories, and the FDA clearing yet another AI diagnostic tool. The money is flowing, but the question everyone is asking behind closed doors is whether the returns will follow.
